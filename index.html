Critical bugs

It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by the readable content of a page when looking at its layout.

E.g.

×

The Facilities Management module is currently down due to technical difficulties - we are working on it!

Tips:
  • Draw attention to the important details by making the time and date bold.
  • Give the user the ability to snooze the announcement, or dismiss it indefinitely.
  • Align the announcement to the very bottom, or very top of the interface so that it does not intrude onto content.
